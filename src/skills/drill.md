# {{SKILL}}: drill

> {{DESC_DRILL}}

**Trigger:** the user wants to *practise* a concept until it is reflex rather than have it explained — "drill me on X", "quiz me until it sticks", "don't explain it, make me use it". For a from-zero overview use [crash-course](crash-course.md); for making confusing material click in the first place use [decode](decode.md).

## Procedure
1. Pin the concept and confirm the user wants to be tested, not lectured. Do not explain the concept up front.
2. Drop them straight into a realistic situation where they would have to use it and would probably make a mistake.
3. When they make a mistake, withhold the answer. Instead ask the one question that forces them to find where their own reasoning broke.
4. Reveal the answer only after they have made at least two honest attempts.
5. Repeat the cycle — new situation each time — until they get it right without hesitation. Then name the variation most likely to catch them out next.

## Done when
- The user works through the situation correctly without hesitation and without being handed the answer.
